short-video-bot

Terms of Service

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Purpose

short-video-bot is an internal creator tool that helps identify public short-form video trends and prepare ranked review reports. It is used for editorial planning and human review.

No public user service

The tool is not offered as a public consumer service. It does not provide public account creation, public TikTok login, or social networking features.

Platform data

When connected to approved platform APIs, short-video-bot may retrieve public metadata about short-form videos, including public engagement metrics and public URLs. The tool does not use platform APIs to download, modify, repost, or remove attribution from platform content.

Editorial responsibility

Operators are responsible for reviewing recommended content, respecting creator rights, obtaining permissions when needed, and complying with applicable platform terms before using any clip in a video project.

Acceptable use

short-video-bot should be used only for lawful trend research, editorial planning, and internal review. It must not be used to access private data, bypass platform restrictions, misrepresent ownership, or automate prohibited platform activity.

Changes

These terms may be updated as the project evolves or as platform requirements change.
